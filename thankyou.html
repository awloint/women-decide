Why are we doing this?

In the elective positions in Nigeria since1999, it is evident that women have not reached 10% Representation from 1999 till date and INEC has emphasized on the urgency for increased participation of women in the electoral processes

African Women In Leadership Organisation in Partnership with YALI Lagos organizes the #WomenDecide Awareness Walk to pass across the message of women's crucial role in electoral decisionmaking.

This is a sensitization walk on women's political participation as Voters and Leaders and a face-to-face interaction with community leaders and stakeholders to spark decision making interest amongst women and girls.
